A Special Note on Author Registration Policy

  • Each accepted full-paper/short-paper/workshop-paper/poster/demo must be accompanied by a Full Registration (not a Student Registration). Each Full Registration can cover up to two (2) full-papers/short-papers/workshop-papers/posters/demos, in any combination.
  • Each accepted full-paper/short-paper/workshop-paper/poster/demo must be presented by one of its author(s) at the conference. The presenting author(s) must be registered for the conference.
  • Student registrations require uploading a scanned student ID card or equivalent verification.
  • All conference registrations include coffee breaks, lunches, the reception, and access to all tutorials.
  • We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express for Early/Late/On-Site registrations and Wire transfers for Early/Late (not on-site) registrations.
  • Registrations can be cancelled by written request to vde-conferences@vde.com no later than August 31, 2017 in which case they are refunded in full minus a 50 EUR processing fee. No refunds are possible after August 31, 2017.
  • We accept only credit cards for On-Site registrations, and do not accept cash.

Side Events

Participation at the side events (T2TRG, RIOT, and ICNRG) is free of charge. However, we kindly you to register in case you want to attend. Registraton is separated from ACM ICN. Please follow the instructions of the side events.
